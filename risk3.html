HK, Mainland China, Macao, Taiwan

You are coming from Mainland China, Macao, or Taiwan (or HK if you chose that for some reason).

For vaccinated people:

Quarantine: 14 days compulsory at a designated place (home, hotel, or other).
Testing: 3 tests during quarantine, and compulsory testing on 16th and 19th day after arrival.
Designated Hotel List for those who choose to stay in a hotel.

For unvaccinated people:

Quarantine: 7 days compulsory at a designated place (home, hotel, or other), and 7 days of self-monitoring after.
Testing: 2 tests during quarantine, and compulsory testing on 9th, 12th, and 16th day after arrival.
Designated Hotel List for those who choose to stay in a hotel.
